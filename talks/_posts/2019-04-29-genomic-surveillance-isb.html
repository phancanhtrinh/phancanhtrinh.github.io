Recently extended to include TB

  • Now takes VCF input, allowing for a variety of bacteria to be included
  • Resistance calls based on genotype
  • Lack of recombination in TB makes it a good target for phylogenetic analysis, things like Klebsiella are much more challenging
  • Examples for global TB evolution and TB in British Columbia, showing preexisting transmitted resistance as well as recent resistance clusters

TB as Trojan Horse to cultivate genomic sequencing in public health

  • Direct sequencing from clinical specimen avoids the multiple week delay of culture
  • Clinically actionable result of treatment
  • Databases of TB genomes to (1) provide critical training data for resistance calls and (2) provide epidemiological context for spread of TB and AMR
